**OHUNSTREAM**

OhunStream is a backend platform I’m building to demonstrate how to design scalable APIs in .NET.

The core idea is a custom dispatcher that decouples request routing from business logic.

This makes the system easier to test, extend, and scale compared to typical controller-heavy designs.
